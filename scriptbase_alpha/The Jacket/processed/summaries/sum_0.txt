The film centers on a wounded Gulf war veteran who returns to his native Vermont suffering from bouts of amnesia.
He is hitching and gets picked up by a stranger, things go pear shaped when a cop pulls them over and is murdered by the stranger.
The vet.
is wrongly accused of killing the cop and lands up in an asylum.
A quack doctor prescribes a course of experimental therapy, restraining him in a heavy duty straight jacket-like device, and locks him away in a body drawer of the basement morgue.
During course of his treatment he gets flashbacks and visions of his future , where he can foresee he is to die in four days time.
The catch is he doesn't know how.
Thus commences the classic race against time.
