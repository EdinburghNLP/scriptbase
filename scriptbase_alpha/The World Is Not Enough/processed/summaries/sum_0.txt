James Bond is back.
An oil tycoon is murdered in MI6 and Bond is sent to protect his daughter.
Renard, who has a bullet lodged in his brain from a previous agent, is secretly planning the destruction of a pipeline.
Bond gains a hand from a research scientist, Dr. Christmas Jones who witnesses the action which happens when Bond meets up with Renard, but Bond becomes suspicious about Elektra King, especially when Bond's boss, M goes missing.
Bond must work quickly to prevent Renard from destroying Europe.
