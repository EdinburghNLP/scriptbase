Based on the classic sci-fi novel by H.G.
Wells, scientist and inventor, Alexander Hartdegen, is determined to prove that time travel is possible.
His determination is turned to desperation by a personal tragedy that now drives him to want to change the past.
Testing his theories with a time machine of his own invention, Hartdegen is hurtled 800,000 years into the future, where he discovers that mankind has divided into the hunter - and the hunted.
