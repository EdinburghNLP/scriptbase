Boy Who Never Slept

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia
Jump to: navigation, search

Boy Who Never Slept is an open-source movie that was released online on July 2, 2006 by Solomon Rothman. It is a comedy with some elements of romance, and is available for download by many means, (including the web and BitTorrent download). The source audio and video files can also be downloaded, so anyone can edit the movie.

Trivia[edit]

  • Boy Who Never Slept was filmed with a budget of $200.
