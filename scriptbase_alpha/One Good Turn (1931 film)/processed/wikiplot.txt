Stan and Ollie are victims of the Great depression and are begging for food.
A friendly old lady provides them with some sandwiches.
Enjoying their meal they hear that the old lady will be thrown out of her house because she is robbed and cannot pay her mortgage.
They don't know that the old lady is rehearsing a play.
Stan and Ollie decide to repay the old lady by selling their car.
During the auction a drunken man (Billy Gilbert) puts a wallet in Stan's pocket.
Ollie accuses Stan of having robbed the old lady but when they return to the old lady's place they hear the truth.
Stan takes revenge on Ollie in a violent way.
