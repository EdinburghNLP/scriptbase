             FADE IN:

             EXT. MEDIEVAL VILLAGE - JUST BEFORE SUNSET

             We see an Italian village at the height of the plague.  Funeral

             moment.

             breaks free of his grasp, starts down the hall.  He laughs a 
             bit, resignedly, and closes the door.
