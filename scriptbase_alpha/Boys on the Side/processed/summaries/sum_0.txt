Jane is a night club singer, out of work.
Robin is a quirky real estate agent looking for a ride-share to accompany her to California.
Her advertisement is answered by Jane, who at first was uncertain about her.
A stop in Pittsburgh picks up a third, Holly, escaping a violent and drug-dealing partner.
Girls on the road, reaching understanding, respect, and care for each other.
But this trio is different - Jane a lesbian, Robin suffering with AIDS, Holly running from her past, seeking one-night stands and a good man.
