Brandon Lang loves football: an injury keeps him from the pros, but his quarterback's anticipation makes him a brilliant predictor of games' outcomes.
Needing money, he leaves Vegas for Manhattan to work for Walter Abrams advising gamblers.
Walter has a doting wife, a young daughter, and a thriving business, but he has problems: a bum heart, a belief he's a master manipulator, and addictions barely kept in check.
He remakes Brandon, and a father-son relationship grows.
Then, things go awry.
Walter may be running a con.
The odds against Brandon mount.
