A millionaire is found dead of heart failure handcuffed to the bed with a home video tape of him and his lover.
When cocaine is found in his system, and his will leaves $8 million to his lover, they arrest her on suspicion of murder.
Her lawyer succumbs to her charms, and he begins a torrid and kinky affair with her.
As new evidence turns up during trial, he begins to wonder if he's defending a murderer.
