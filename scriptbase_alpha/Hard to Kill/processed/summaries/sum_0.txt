Mason Storm, a 'go it alone' cop, is gunned down at home.
The intruders kill his wife, and think they've killed both Mason and his son too.
Mason is secretly taken to a hospital where he spends several years in a coma.
His son meanwhile is growing up thinking his father is dead.
When Mason wakes up, everyone is in danger - himself, his son, his best friend, his nurse - but most of all those who arranged for his death
